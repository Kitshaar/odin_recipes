Tomato Cucumber Kachumber



Description

Tomato cucumber kachumber is a refreshing and vibrant Indian salad that showcases the beautiful combination of tomatoes and cucumbers. This light and healthy dish is popularly served as a side dish or accompaniment with various Indian meals. The salad features a colorful medley of diced tomatoes and cucumbers, mixed with finely chopped onions, green chilies, and fresh cilantro. The flavors are further enhanced by the addition of tangy lemon juice, aromatic cumin powder, and a hint of spicy red chili powder. The result is a delightful blend of textures and tastes, with the crispness of the cucumbers contrasting with the juicy sweetness of the tomatoes. Tomato cucumber kachumber is not only visually appealing but also a perfect balance of flavors, making it a perfect choice for those seeking a light and refreshing dish.

This simple yet delicious salad offers a multitude of health benefits. Tomatoes are packed with vitamins A, C, and E, along with essential antioxidants that promote healthy skin and boost the immune system. Cucumbers, on the other hand, are known for their high water content, helping to keep the body hydrated and aiding in digestion. Additionally, the combination of tomatoes and cucumbers provides a good dose of dietary fiber, promoting a healthy digestive system. With its vibrant colors, refreshing flavors, and numerous health benefits, tomato cucumber kachumber is a popular choice for a quick and nutritious salad that can be enjoyed year-round.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup halved grape tomatoes
  • cup diced cucumber
  • ½ cup chopped green onions
  • ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mix grape tomatoes, cucumber, green onions, cilantro, lemon juice, mint, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl.
